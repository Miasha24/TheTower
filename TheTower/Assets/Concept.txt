Defend your tower!

Every round you can choose one of three upgrades

Try combine upgrades that synergize to survive as long as possible!



--------

Different upgrades scale based off of 1 of 3 base stats: Offensive, Defensive, Utility

Offensive scale upgrades that aim to destroy enemies before they even get close to your tower
Examples of Offensive upgrades: Multishot, Cluster Bombs, Missiles

Defensive scale upgrades that helps your tower survive when enemies gets close
Examples of Defensive upgrades: Sword, Shield, Mines

Utility scale upgrades that hinder and hurt your enemies in a variaty of different ways
Examples of Utility upgrades: Frostbite, Mini-Towers, 

Increasing a base stat costs coins, and no matter what stat is increased, all of them will increase in price.
